FAQ

What type of flashlights do you sell?

We offer a wide range of flashlights, including LED flashlights, tactical flashlights, rechargeable models, and specialty flashlights for outdoor activities, emergencies, and professional use.

Are your flashlights waterproof?

Many of our flashlights are waterproof or water-resistant. Check the product specifications for each flashlight to see its water resistance rating (e.g., IPX7 or IPX8).

What is the warranty on your flashlights?

We provide a one-year warranty on most of our flashlights, covering manufacturing defects. Please review the product details for specific warranty information.

How do I choose the right flashlight?

Consider factors like brightness (measured in lumens), battery type (rechargeable or disposable), size, and purpose (e.g., outdoor use, professional tasks). If you need help, feel free to contact us for recommendations!
